Fort Myers' Only Youth Training Facility Built Around Measurable Results

Redline Athletics on Cleveland Avenue is not another gym with cones and a whistle. Every session follows a five-pillar methodology. Every athlete gets quarterly testing across six standardized assessments. Every coach is certified to develop the specific athletic qualities that show up on game day.

Led by General Manager Chase Stehling and a team of performance coaches, this facility pairs AI-powered motion analysis with hands-on coaching that builds speed, strength, confidence, and character in young athletes across Fort Myers, Cape Coral, Lehigh Acres, and Estero.

Quarterly performance testing across 6 standardized metrics
Demotu 3D motion capture for biomechanical analysis

Inside a Redline Training Session

Every session follows the same five-pillar progression, so your athlete develops complete, well-rounded athleticism instead of just getting tired.

Activation

Targeted injury prevention and muscle activation protocols

Dynamic Warmup

Movement prep that primes the nervous system for peak output

Speed & Agility

Sprint mechanics, lateral quickness, and change-of-direction drills

Strength & Power

Age-appropriate resistance training for functional, explosive strength

Mobility & Recovery

Flexibility and cooldown to improve range and accelerate recovery

We train athletes from ages 3 through 18. Our Intro to Sports program serves ages 3-7, while our Flex and Elite memberships are designed for athletes ages 8-18. We also offer Adult Fitness programs for ages 18+.
No prior experience is needed. Whether your child is just starting out or competing at an elite level, our certified coaches customize training to their current ability and goals.
Every quarter, athletes complete six standardized assessments: vertical jump, broad jump, 20-yard dash, pro agility test, bench press, and pull-ups. Results are tracked in our mobile app so you can see measurable improvement over time.
Athletes who train consistently for 90 days see an average 17.16% increase in power, 13.89% increase in speed, and 10.38% increase in explosiveness, as measured by quarterly performance testing.
Flex is our most popular program. Sessions start every hour, and athletes ages 8-18 can choose unlimited sessions, single sessions, or away weeks when traveling for competitions. It's built around the schedule of a multi-sport kid.
Redline is sport-agnostic. We train foundational athletic qualities, speed, agility, strength, power, and mobility, that transfer to every sport. Our athletes play football, baseball, soccer, basketball, volleyball, lacrosse, tennis, swimming, track, and more.
