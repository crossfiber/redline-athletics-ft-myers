Our Method

Every Session, Five Pillars

Each training session is structured around five core pillars, ensuring athletes develop complete, well-rounded athleticism instead of just getting tired.

Activation & Injury Prevention

Targeted warmup protocols that prepare the body and reduce injury risk before intense training begins.

Dynamic Warmup

Movement-based preparation that elevates heart rate and primes the nervous system for peak performance.

Speed & Agility

Sprint mechanics, lateral quickness, and change-of-direction drills that directly transfer to game-day speed.

Strength & Power

Age-appropriate resistance training building functional strength, explosive power, and athletic confidence.

Mobility & Recovery

Flexibility work and cooldown protocols that improve range of motion and accelerate recovery between sessions.

Common Questions

We train athletes from ages 3 through 18. Our Intro to Sports program serves ages 3-7, while our Flex and Elite memberships are designed for athletes ages 8-18. We also offer Adult Fitness programs for ages 18+.
No prior experience is needed. Our programs are designed to meet athletes where they are. Whether your child is just starting out or competing at an elite level, our certified coaches customize training to their ability.
Every quarter, athletes complete standardized assessments including vertical jump, broad jump, 20-yard dash, pro agility test, bench press, and pull-ups. Results are tracked in our mobile app so you can see measurable improvement over time.
